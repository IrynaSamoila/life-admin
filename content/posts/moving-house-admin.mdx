Moving house generates enormous admin. Much of it has deadlines. Missing tasks costs money or causes problems.

Here's every administrative task, organised by when to do it.

This checklist focuses on practical admin tasks that prevent service interruptions, missed deadlines, and unnecessary fees during your move.

## The Timeline Overview

**8 weeks before:** Start preparation, gather information
**6 weeks before:** Begin notifications, arrange services
**4 weeks before:** Handle utilities, financial updates
**2 weeks before:** Final notifications, address changes
**1 week before:** Verification, last-minute tasks
**Moving day:** Essential tasks only
**After move:** Completion and cleanup

Different moves have different timelines. Adjust this template to your situation.

## 8 Weeks Before Move

Early preparation makes everything else easier.

### Research New Area

**Understand your new location:**
- Council tax band (check council website)
- Rubbish collection days (impacts moving day planning)
- Parking restrictions (for moving van)
- Local services (doctors, dentists, schools)

**Time required:** 1-2 hours, one sitting

### Gather Current Information

**Compile list of all:**
- Utility providers and account numbers
- Insurance policies
- Subscriptions (delivery box, streaming, magazines)
- Memberships (gym, clubs, professional)
- Services tied to address (cleaner, gardener, regular deliveries)

**Why now:** Easier to find when you're not packing boxes.

**Time required:** 30-45 minutes

### Notify Landlord (If Renting)

**If leaving rental:**
- Check notice period in tenancy agreement
- Provide written notice to landlord
- Request deposit return process
- Book end-of-tenancy cleaning if required
- Schedule checkout inspection

**Critical:** Many tenancies require 1-2 months notice. Check your contract.

### Book Removal Company (If Using)

**If you need professional movers:**
- Get 3 quotes
- Check reviews
- Verify insurance coverage
- Book preferred company
- Confirm date in writing

Good removal companies book up 4-8 weeks in advance.

## 6 Weeks Before Move

Start notifying organizations and arranging services.

### Arrange Utilities at New Address

**Contact providers for:**

**Electricity and Gas:**
- Find current supplier for new property
- Arrange switch or new account
- Set start date (day of move)
- Provide meter readings if possible

**Water:**
- Find water supplier for new area (usually regional monopoly)
- Set up account
- Provide start date

**Internet/Broadband:**
- Check what's available at new address
- Order service (installation can take 2-4 weeks)
- Schedule installation date (soon after move)
- Consider keeping old service active until new works

**Time required:** 1-2 hours total for all utilities

### Update Electoral Roll

**Register at new address:**
- Visit gov.uk/register-to-vote
- Provide new address
- Provide move date

**Remove from old address:**
- Happens automatically when you register at new address

**Why it matters:**
- Required for credit applications
- Affects credit score
- Needed for jury duty, voting

**Time required:** 10 minutes

### Notify Council Tax

**New address:**
- Contact new local council
- Provide move-in date
- Set up payment (usually direct debit)
- May need to pay upfront for first month

**Old address:**
- Contact old local council
- Provide move-out date
- Settle final bill
- Stop payment after final bill

**Critical:** You're liable for council tax from your move date, even if house is empty while you settle in.

**Time required:** 20-30 minutes per council

## 4 Weeks Before Move

Financial updates and more notifications.

### Update Bank Accounts

**For all bank accounts:**
- Online banking: Update address
- Request new cards to be sent to new address after move date
- Update direct debit address if different from correspondence address

**Consider timing:**
- Some banks send new cards automatically when address changes
- Time this so cards arrive after you've moved

**Time required:** 15 minutes per bank

### Update Credit Cards

**For each credit card:**
- Change billing address
- Consider setting new address active from move date
- Verify direct debits will continue

**Time required:** 10 minutes per card

### Notify Insurance Providers

**Update address for:**

**Home Insurance:**
- Notify old address end date
- Set up new policy for new address (often required before exchange/move)
- Get quote 4 weeks ahead for best rates

**Car Insurance:**
- Provide new address
- Confirm if premium changes (often does)
- Update effective from move date

**Life/Health Insurance:**
- Update address for correspondence

**Time required:** 15-20 minutes per policy

### Update Driving License

**DVLA notification:**
- Visit gov.uk/change-address-driving-licence
- Free online update
- Receive new license in 1-2 weeks

**Or:**
- Write new address on paper counterpart (if you still have one)

**Critical:** Legal requirement to update within 3 weeks of moving.

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Update Vehicle Registration

**If you own a vehicle:**
- Visit gov.uk/change-vehicle-details-registration-certificate
- Free online update
- Receive new V5C at new address

**Critical:** Legal requirement, must do within 4 weeks.

**Time required:** 10 minutes

## 3 Weeks Before Move

Subscription and service updates.

### Redirect Mail

**Royal Mail Redirection:**
- Visit royalmail.com/redirection
- Choose 3, 6, or 12 month redirection
- Costs Â£36-85 depending on duration
- Set start date as move date
- Covers household or individuals

**Worth it:** Catches mail you forget to redirect.

**Time required:** 10 minutes to set up

### Update Delivery Subscriptions

**For regular deliveries:**
- Milk delivery
- Newspaper delivery
- Meal kit subscriptions
- Regular grocery deliveries

**Process:**
- Cancel at old address (provide last delivery date)
- Set up at new address (provide first delivery date)

**Time required:** 5 minutes per subscription

### Notify Employer

**Provide to HR/payroll:**
- New address
- Effective date
- Update emergency contact if phone changes

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Update GP Registration

**At old surgery:**
- Notify you're leaving
- Request medical records transfer

**At new surgery:**
- Register as new patient (can do after move)
- Provide new address
- Medical records will transfer automatically

**Consider:** If moving far, do this after move to ensure you're in catchment area.

**Time required:** 15 minutes per surgery

### Update Dentist

**If changing dentist:**
- Notify current dentist of leaving
- Research NHS dentists in new area
- Register when you arrive (if accepting new patients)

**If keeping same dentist:** Update address for records

**Time required:** 10-15 minutes

## 2 Weeks Before Move

Final notifications and preparations.

### Update Online Shopping Accounts

**Major retailers:**
- Amazon
- Supermarkets with regular orders
- Any stores with saved addresses

**Process:**
- Log in to each account
- Update default delivery address
- Consider setting new address to activate on move date

**Time required:** 15-20 minutes for all accounts

### Notify Children's Schools

**If children changing schools:**
- Notify current school of leaving date
- Provide new school information
- Request records transfer
- Register at new school

**If staying at same school:**
- Update address with school office

**Time required:** 30 minutes - 1 hour

### Update Pension Providers

**For all pension schemes:**
- Workplace pensions
- Private pensions
- State pension (via HMRC)

**Update correspondence address**

**Time required:** 10 minutes per provider

### Update Memberships

**Professional:**
- Professional body memberships
- Industry associations
- Trade unions

**Personal:**
- Gym membership (cancel or transfer)
- Club memberships
- Library card

**Time required:** 5 minutes per membership

### Meter Readings

**Book for moving day:**
- Old property: Final readings when you hand over keys
- New property: Initial readings when you receive keys

**Take photos:**
- Visual proof of readings
- Prevents billing disputes
- Date stamp shows when reading taken

**Time required:** 5 minutes per property on moving day

## 1 Week Before Move

Final verification and last-minute tasks.

### Confirm All Arrangements

**Verify:**
- Removal company booked (if using)
- Van booked (if self-moving)
- Time and date confirmed
- Contact number for moving day

**Time required:** 10 minutes

### Defrost Freezer

**At old property:**
- Use up frozen food or donate
- Defrost freezer 24-48 hours before move
- Clean and dry
- Prevents water damage in transit

**Time required:** Passive, but requires planning

### Cancel Old Utilities

**For old property:**
- Set end date for all utilities
- Provide forwarding address for final bills
- Take final meter readings (moving day)

**Don't cancel too early:**
- Need electricity for cleaning
- Need water for final cleanup
- Keep active until you hand over keys

**Time required:** 15-20 minutes total

### Pack Essentials Box

**For immediate needs:**
- Kettle, mugs, tea/coffee
- Basic toiletries
- Phone chargers
- Important documents
- Medications
- Change of clothes
- Basic tools

**Label clearly:** "OPEN FIRST"

**Time required:** 20 minutes

## Moving Day

Essential tasks only. Everything else is already done.

### Old Property - When Leaving

**Before final walkthrough:**
- Take final meter readings (photo evidence)
- Text/email readings to suppliers immediately
- Do final check of all rooms, cupboards, shed, garage
- Take photos of property condition

**When handing over keys:**
- Confirm meter readings with landlord/agent
- Get receipt for key handover
- Note time and date

### New Property - When Arriving

**Immediately on arrival:**
- Take photos of property condition
- Take initial meter readings (photo evidence)
- Text/email readings to suppliers
- Test basics: lights, water, heating

**If anything wrong:**
- Document immediately
- Photo evidence
- Notify landlord/seller same day

### Practical Tasks

**Moving day priorities:**
- Unpack essentials box first
- Set up beds
- Locate emergency shutoff valves (water, gas, electric)
- Check heating works
- Identify fuse box location

## First Week After Move

Completion tasks and settling in.

### Verify Utilities Working

**Test all services:**
- Electricity (all rooms)
- Gas (if applicable)
- Water (hot and cold)
- Heating
- Broadband (when installed)

**Report issues immediately:** First week is when problems typically appear.

### Update HMRC

**For tax purposes:**
- Visit gov.uk/tell-hmrc-change-of-details
- Update address for:
  - Self-assessment
  - Child benefit
  - Tax credits
- Or call HMRC if preferred

**Time required:** 10-15 minutes

### Update DWP (If Applicable)

**If receiving benefits:**
- Universal Credit
- Pension Credit
- Disability benefits

**Notify immediately:** Benefit payments may stop if address not updated.

**Time required:** 15 minutes

### Update TV Licence

**Process:**
- Visit tvlicensing.co.uk/cs/change-address
- Provide new address
- Update payment details if needed
- License transfers to new address

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Complete Missing Notifications

**Go through original list:**
- Check each item updated
- Action any missed
- Verify confirmations received

## First Month After Move

Final cleanup and verification.

### Verify All Bills Updated

**Check you're receiving bills at new address:**
- Utilities
- Council tax
- Insurance
- Credit cards
- Bank statements

**If not receiving:** Contact provider to verify address updated.

### Update Passport

**Process:**
- Not automatic - optional update
- Can update when renewing
- Or update via passport office (costs money)

**Consider:** Only necessary if you move frequently or travel often.

### Update Wi-Fi Password

**On new router:**
- Change default password
- Update connected devices
- Set up network name

**Time required:** 15 minutes

### Register with Local Services

**Non-urgent but useful:**
- Local library
- Local pharmacy
- Recycling centre (permit if required)
- Parking permits (if applicable)

## Time Investment Summary

**Pre-move admin:** 5-8 hours total spread over 8 weeks
**Moving day tasks:** 30-60 minutes
**Post-move admin:** 2-3 hours over first month

**Total:** 8-12 hours of administrative tasks for typical move

Spread over 10 weeks, this is manageable.

## What Happens If You Forget

**Forgotten tasks cause:**
- Missed bills (late fees)
- Service interruptions
- Legal issues (driving license, vehicle registration)
- Credit score impacts (missed electoral roll)
- Lost mail
- Utility account issues

Use the checklist. Print it. Don't rely on memory.

## Digital Tools

**Simple spreadsheet works:**
- Copy checklist to spreadsheet
- Add completion dates
- Add notes and reference numbers
- Track all in one place

**Calendar reminders:**
- Set reminder for each deadline
- Include what needs doing
- Link to checklist item

No need for specialized moving apps. Checklist + calendar is sufficient.

Moving house is administratively complex. But it's not complicated if you have a complete checklist and follow it systematically.

Print the checklist. Start 8 weeks before. Tick off items as completed.

Boring. Thorough. Stress-free move.
